About Swarna Swayam Krushi Society

Supported by Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment, Government of India (Disabled Affairs)

Our Vision

To create a nurturing environment where every child with physical and mental disabilities receives comprehensive care, education, and therapy to reach their fullest potential and live with dignity.

Our Mission

Providing specialized residential care, individualized education programs, and therapeutic services for physically and mentally challenged children while supporting their families and promoting inclusion in society.

Key Achievements

  • Established residential school serving 150+ special children
  • Provided therapy services to 300+ children
  • Supported 200+ families with disabled children
  • Trained 50+ special education teachers
